Hi there, I am Poonam, and I fell into Photography in the Summer of 2014 and have been super grateful ever since. I run my business from my home office based right outside of Mumbai, from a place alternatively known as "the City of Lakes". Besides Photography, I am a big fan of management books, really long walks, beaches, fresh flowers and let me stress on this one, I am obsessed with limbs, okay! I warned you.



YOU by Poonam Kotecha, is a personal expression to my concept of beauty. {I RELY ON ACCIDNETS A GREAT DEAL AND ALSO ON CONTINUITY... I pRE] what I find where I am determined to make you feel at your most comfortable because that's when I find you extremely beautiful. After experimenting with quite a few styles, I realized my heart skips a beat every time I shoot raw emotions, messy moments and the in-betweens. I want to leave you smiling aimlessly even before we finish, or with a terrible tummy ache due to excessive laughter! Of course, I can only do what I do, because of my client's complete trust in me.


WEDDINGS by Poonam Kotecha, is a team effort and I work with a tiny team and I tend to call them my children. I don't like to crowd your wedding with my team, we try to be invisible or blend in [whatever seems right at the moment]. I also don't take up weddings when multiple teams are covering it. We like cozy settings and big laughter, and our photographs are just the reflection of that, intimate & honest. So far, we have been blessed with beauty, laughter, grace and sometimes, a jazz concert kind of a vibe which turns into a mad-rave in no time.

1. You are here.

By now you must have seen the photographs and read the about me and you are still here. Welcome! I am glad my work spoke to you, in the spirit of that let's take a quick walk-through. To begin with let me first handle your no.1 query, "you don't know how to pose". I hear you and I can assure you everyone feels that way for the first 10 minutes since this isn't an everyday activity for most of us. And that works in our favor the most since the photographs will come out fun, raw and real - just the way you are. And I'll be there to guide you along the way. Secondly, it helps to get shot before the wedding, for starters, you'll both know what to do during the wedding.


If you have made your decision, go right ahead and fill in the details for me here, put in as much information as you can, and I'll get back with an custom quote for your wedding.


2. The Couple's Shoot.

Iusually have a consultation session with you on skype or through phone, a little bit of getting to know each other. We decide on the location, wardrobe & other details as per my style and your vision. During the couple's shoot, you take some time off the wedding planning and running-around, relax! You be at your messy/natural/imperfect best looking absolutely fine and I take a few pictures. And more than often, you will want me to take more photos.


3. Pre-wedding Planning.

When you have all the details ready, we speak on the phone/ through email and prepare a time-line for your wedding, you tell me about the final timings & flow of the ceremonies, friends and families not to be missed. One of my bride made a Power-point presentation of all the important people she didn't want me to miss. Get creative! If it's a destination wedding, here is when you book the tickets for my team and make arrangements for our stay. Show me the photographs of the venue, your wardrobe, his wardrobe, the decor so I have an idea of what I am shooting during the wedding and prepare my team for the same.


4. The Wedding Day.

You're getting married!! Yay!! You introduce us to your family and friends, make sure you give us enough time to shoot you two with them and then some more time to shoot just the two of you, the more the better!! I want to give you a great experience not just the photos, so all-in-all have a great time!


5. The Photographs.

Yt's important to me that I deliver photographs that you can show-off proudly year after year. I edit the images and deliver through an Online Gallery between 6-8 weeks of the wedding. If you decide to hang it on your wall or create an album, you can do so once you receive the final gallery. I have made it extremely easy for you to select the images you'd want in your album/s with zero hassle. Just mark them as your favorite in the gallery, give me a heads up and I'll print an album for you in 2 weeks and there you go, your story in PRINT!
